About Us

MODdy was developed by the https://github.com/AY1920S2-CS2103T-W13-3 team.

MODdy is a way for NUS students to manage the modules that they have taken, are taking or plan to take during the course of their education. MODdy handles module management as well as keeping track of the student’s grades for each of the modules.

We are a team based in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore.
